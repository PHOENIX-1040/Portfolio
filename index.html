§ 02 — In Development

An unfinished experiment.

Active

AR Maintenance & Assembly Assistant for Industrial Equipment

A self-initiated Augmented Reality application built in Unity with AR Foundation, targeting industrial maintenance workflows. The concept: a technician points their phone at a piece of machinery (a Bosch VE injection pump in my prototype), and the app overlays the 3D model, lets them explode it into its component parts, and walks them through assembly or disassembly with step-by-step text hints and animated cues for screws, bolts, and sub-assemblies.

What works today is the foundation: the 3D model is imported and structured into 127 individually addressable sub-components, and AR placement onto real surfaces runs on an Android device. What doesn't yet, and what defines the project's actual concept, is everything that turns this from "a 3D model in AR" into an assistant. The explode-toggle interaction is scaffolded but not functional. Overlaying the model onto a real physical pump is unbuilt. The step-by-step guidance layer with animated cues for screws and bolts is not started. Measured against the original concept, the project is roughly 25 to 30 percent complete. The defining features are still ahead of me.

I'm listing this deliberately, unfinished, because it's the most honest signal I can give about why I want a Master's. I've taken it as far as self-teaching, YouTube, and Unity docs will carry me. A structured programme with peers, supervision, and access to AR/VR hardware is precisely the bridge I'm reaching for.

01. 2026 Final Year Project Vision Transformers

Intelligent Violence Detection with DeiT & Temporal Consistency

Why this matters for immersive tech: at its core, this is a spatial-temporal video understanding problem. The same family of techniques powers gesture recognition, gaze tracking, and human-presence detection in XR systems.

A real-time campus surveillance system built on a Data-Efficient Image Transformer (DeiT) backbone with a temporal-consistency layer that smooths frame-level predictions into reliable event detections. The goal is to overcome the well-documented attention decay of human CCTV operators (which drops sharply after 15 to 20 minutes) and the chronic false-positive rate of motion-based heuristics, flagging genuine threats with low latency while keeping benign activity quiet.

02. 2025 IEEE Publication UAV · Aerial Sensing

Drone-Based Precision Agriculture using Machine Learning

Why this matters for immersive tech: aerial scene understanding, spatial sensing, and large-scale environmental mapping are the same primitives that drive outdoor AR, digital twins, and geospatial XR.

A solar-powered DJI Mavic 2 Pro framework that captures 4K aerial imagery of farmland and routes it through cloud-based deep learning pipelines to automate four critical tasks: water-scarcity detection, weed mapping, crop disease identification with treatment recommendations, and growth-stage yield prediction. The system delivers real-time spatial insights to farmers through a mobile interface, replacing the cost and rigidity of conventional IoT-only setups.

03. 2024 NLP · RAG OOSE

Data RAG — Retrieval-Augmented Document Intelligence

Why this matters: semantic retrieval underpins voice agents, narrative AI, and contextual assistants. The kind of intelligence layer that makes immersive environments respond meaningfully to the user inside them.

A modular retrieval-augmented generation framework that ingests heterogeneous corpora (PDFs, text, structured sources), chunks and embeds them via Sentence-BERT / MiniLM, and serves semantic queries through a FAISS vector index with cosine similarity. The architecture cleanly separates ingestion, embedding, vector storage, query handling, and UI, designed as an OOSE artifact with full test coverage for ambiguity, edge-case data, and real-time responsiveness.

04. 2025 Cybersecurity Applied ML

AI-Driven Network Security Dashboard

Less directly relevant to XR, but included as evidence of breadth across ML domains, and a reminder that immersive systems will eventually need their own trust and integrity layers.

A hybrid intrusion detection framework that unifies UDP Flood (DDoS) and ARP Spoofing detection into a single real-time monitoring dashboard. Two Random Forest classifiers run in parallel on independently engineered feature sets, with their outputs fused at the dashboard layer to visualise risk probabilities, alert urgency, and live traffic analytics, replacing the brittle signature-matching of traditional IDS.
